The training and testing sets used for program synthesis problems can be found on GitHub: https://github.com/amlalejini/GECCO-2024-phylogeny-informed-subsampling/tree/main/experiments/2023-12-30-psynth/hpc/config.

All of our experimental data is available online from our OSF respository: https://osf.io/h3f52/
